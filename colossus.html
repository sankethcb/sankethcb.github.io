Project Colossus

WIP Gameplay Demo

Description

In the year 2255, robots have taken over the earth. The resistance to the robot revolution have planned an attack on the factory that is currently building a robot colossus. Will the half built colossus destroy the resistanceâ€™s attack? Or will the resistance be able to destroy the colossus.

Gameplay

One player takes control of the robot colossus through the tethered virtual reality headset. The other two players use controllers through a PC to play as resistance attempting to destroy the Colossus. The resistance fires their weapons and uses their abilities to attack the colossus in hopes of destroying it. The Colossus throws objects and uses abilities to hit Resistance in hopes to lower their health and kill them.

Details

  • Developed in Unity3D utilizing OpenVR and SteamVR.
  • As one of the lead programmers my role was to develop all soldier behaviours including movement and the varied class abilities and weapons.
