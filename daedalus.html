Daedalus

Description

A 1v1 board game based on the Greek legend of Daedalusâ€™s labyrinth. Each player must construct their own labyrinth at the beginning of the game, and get their gladiator to the other side of the board. Every turn, players are allowed to move their gladiator after shifting their labyrinth blocks. To defend themselves, players move their Minotaur every other turn to try and corner the enemy gladiator.
